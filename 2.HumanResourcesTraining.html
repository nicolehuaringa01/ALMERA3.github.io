Full-Time Trained Professionals

Key Points

  • Europe has the highest number of laboratories across all categories, indicating a strong presence and capacity for trained professionals.
  • Africa and the Middle East have fewer laboratories overall, with minimal representation in the higher categories.
  • The majority of the laboratories fall within the 1-5 and 6-10 trained professional categories, suggesting that most labs operate with a relatively small team of trained professionals.
