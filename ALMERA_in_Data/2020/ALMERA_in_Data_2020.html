ALMERA in Data (2020)

Welcome to the ALMERA in Data platform, designed to provide comprehensive insights derived from the ALMERA network's capabilities survey.

The 2020 survey round successfully concluded with 87 submitted responses.

It is important to note that while 186 laboratories were invited, not all participating laboratories provided responses for every survey question, which may influence the comprehensiveness of certain data points.
