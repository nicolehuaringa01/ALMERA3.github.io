3. Equipment Availability

This pie chart illustrates the types and relative abundance of analytical equipment owned by laboratories within the ALMERA network. Gamma-ray spectrometry systems represent the most widely used instrumentation. Alpha spectrometry systems and liquid scintillation counters also make up significant portions.

The number in parentheses next to each laboratory name indicates how many units of that specific equipment type the laboratory possesses.
