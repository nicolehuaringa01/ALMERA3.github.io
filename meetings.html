ALMERA Coordination Meetings

21st Coordination Meeting (2024) in Rabat, Morocco.

The Annual Coordination Meetings of the ALMERA network take place usually in October or November to assess the network's status and to define its future activities. The host is rotated among different regional groups on the network. IAEA may partially subsidize participation to increase interaction between laboratories. The minutes, outcomes and recommendations of the meeting will be published in newsletters.
