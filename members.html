ALMERA Network Laboratories

As of June 2025 the ALMERA network consists of 200 laboratories representing 90 member states. Two additional laboratories from the IAEA Physical and Chemical Sciences division include the Terrestrial Environmental Radiochemistry (TERC) laboratory in Seibersdorf, Austria, and the Marine Environmental Laboratories in Monaco (Central Coordinator).

Membership Criteria

  • Laboratories must be nominated by their respective governments via the Permanent Mission to the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA).
  • Membership is open to multiple laboratories per country with no nomination deadline.
